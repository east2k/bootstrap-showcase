Responsive Images

applies max-width: 100% and height: auto

Code:

                                    
                                        <img src="placeholder-image.png" class="img-fluid" alt="...">

Image Thumbnail

applies rounded 1px border

Code:

                                    
                                        <img src="small-placeholder-image.png" class="img-fluid" alt="...">

Aligning Images w/ Float

Code:

                                    
                                        <img src="small-placeholder-image.png" class="rounded float-start" alt="...">
                                        <img src="small-placeholder-image.png" class="rounded float-end" alt="...">

Aligning Images w/ Margin

Code:

                                    
                                        <img src="small-placeholder-image.png" class="rounded mx-auto d-block" alt="...">

Aligning Images w/ Text-Center

Code:

                                    
                                        <div class="text-center">
